Hello and welcome to the political debate! Your goal here is to attack your opponent till his health falls to 0, you will face 1 out of 3 politicians in this round! Who you will get is random, but each one has a unique bonus to their randomized stats!

The game is turn based, and here you have to set what you want your stats to be, if you increase your deception (lie) you will generate more damage and get more damage boosts each turn. In fact, if you set your deception to 8 or above, you will always be granted a chance (1/3) to multiply your damage by 1.5!

Your honesty will give you a better chance to survive your opponent's attack! However, if it's too low, your defense is rather weak. However, If your honesty is set to 10, your opponent's damage will ALWAYS be halfed!
